Tayra (Eira barbara)

Characteristics

Large mustelid (up to 6 kg), long body, dark fur with a lighter head.

Habitat and distribution

Forests of Central and South America. Relatively common in Costa Rica.

Behavior

Diurnal, omnivorous, and very agile. Climbs trees and hunts small animals.

Conservation status

Least Concern.
