Juanilama (Lippia alba)

Characteristics

Aromatic shrub with leaves commonly used for making teas and infusions.

Habitat and Distribution

Found in tropical regions of the Americas; common in Costa Rica.

Behavior

Prefers moist, well-drained soils and typically blooms during the summer.

Conservation Status

Not threatened.

Where to find it?

Frequently cultivated in home gardens and orchards.

Fun Facts

  • Traditionally used to relieve digestive issues and nervous conditions.
