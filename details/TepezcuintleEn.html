Tepezcuintle or Paca (Cuniculus paca)

Characteristics

Large rodent (up to 10 kg), dark fur with white side spots.

Habitat and distribution

Humid forests near bodies of water from Mexico to South America.

Behavior

Nocturnal, solitary, very stealthy.

Conservation status

Least Concern; hunted for its meat.
