Scarlet Macaw (Ara macao)

Characteristics

Large macaw with bright red plumage and blue and yellow wings.

Habitat and Distribution

Two main populations in Costa Rica: one in the Osa Conservation Area and another in the Central Pacific Conservation Area; smaller groups can be found in other regions.

Behavior

They form lifelong monogamous pairs and feed on fruits and seeds.

Conservation Status

Endangered (EN) according to the IUCN; threats include habitat loss and illegal trade.
