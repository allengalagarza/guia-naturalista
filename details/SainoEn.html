Saino or Collared Peccary (Pecari tajacu)

Characteristics

Similar to a wild boar, with a white "collar" around the neck, up to 25 kg.

Habitat and distribution

Forests, shrublands, and dry areas from southern USA to Argentina.

Behavior

Lives in groups up to 20 individuals, very social. Diurnal or crepuscular.

Conservation status

Least Concern.
