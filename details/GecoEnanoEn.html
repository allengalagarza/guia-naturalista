Dwarf Yellow-spotted Gecko (Lepidoblepharis xanthostigma)

Characteristics

Small brown gecko with yellow spots; measures about 3-4 cm in length.

Habitat and Distribution

Found in Nicaragua, Costa Rica, Panama, and Colombia, inhabiting lowland humid forests.

Behavior

Nocturnal and terrestrial; feeds on small insects; lays single eggs in leaf litter.

Conservation Status

Least Concern (LC) according to the IUCN.
