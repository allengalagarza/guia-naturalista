Yellow-naped Amazon (Amazona auropalliata)

Characteristics

Medium-sized parrot with green plumage and a distinctive yellow patch on the nape.

Lives in dry forests and mangroves along Costa Ricaâ€™s Pacific coast.

Behavior

Social and noisy; feeds on seeds, fruits, and nuts.

Conservation Status

Critically Endangered (CR) according to the IUCN; populations are rapidly declining.

Where to find it?

Dry forests in northwestern Costa Rica, though its numbers have decreased significantly. Notable tourist reserves in Nicoya: Curu Reserve and Cabo Blanco.

Fun Facts

  • It is well known for its ability to mimic sounds and human voices.
