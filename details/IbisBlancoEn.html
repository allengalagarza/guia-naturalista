White Ibis (Eudocimus albus)

Characteristics

White bird with a long downward-curved bill and pink legs.

Habitat and Distribution

Common resident in the lowlands of both Costa Rican coasts, especially abundant in the Gulf of Nicoya and the Tempisque basin.

Behavior

Forages in groups, walking and searching for food in shallow waters.

Conservation Status

Least Concern (LC) according to the IUCN.

Where to find it?

Mangroves, estuaries, and wetlands of the Pacific and Caribbean coasts of Costa Rica. Notable tourist reserves in Nicoya: Curu Reserve and Cabo Blanco.

Fun Facts

  • Often seen in large flocks, especially while feeding.
