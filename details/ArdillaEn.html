Variegated Squirrel (Sciurus variegatoides)

Characteristics

Large squirrel with variable colors (red, gray, black) and a very bushy tail.

Habitat and Distribution

From southern Mexico to Panama. Very common in Costa Rica.

Behavior

Diurnal and arboreal; feeds on fruits, nuts, and bark.

Conservation Status

Least Concern.

Where to find it?

In forests, rural areas, and urban parks. Notable tourist reserves in Nicoya: Karen Mogensen Nature Reserve, Curu Reserve, and Cabo Blanco.

Fun Facts

  • Highly adaptable, even in human environments.
