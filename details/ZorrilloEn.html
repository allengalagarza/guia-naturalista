Zorrillo (Petiveria alliacea)

Characteristics

Herbaceous plant with a strong smell similar to garlic.

Habitat and distribution

Tropical areas of America, common in Costa Rica.

Medicinal uses

Anti-inflammatory, for infections, purgative, rheumatism.

Where to find it?

Road edges, gardens, vacant lots. Notable tourist reserves in Nicoya: Cabo Blanco Absolute Natural Reserve

Fun facts

  • In traditional medicine, it is attributed to relieving stomach pain.
