Sábila (Aloe vera)

Characteristics

Succulent plant with fleshy leaves full of gel.

Habitat and distribution

Native to North Africa; widely cultivated in tropical zones.

Behavior

Drought resistant; grows in sandy and well-drained soils.

Conservation status

Not threatened.

Where to find it?

Grown in gardens and pots.

Fun facts

  • The gel from its leaves is used to treat burns and skin conditions.
