Ocelot (Leopardus pardalis)

Characteristics

Medium-sized feline (up to 15 kg), yellowish fur with black rosette-shaped spots.

Habitat and distribution

Tropical forests, mangroves, and dense vegetation areas from Mexico to Argentina. Present throughout Costa Rica.

Behavior

Nocturnal, solitary, territorial; hunts small mammals, birds, and reptiles.

Conservation status

Least Concern (IUCN), but locally threatened by habitat fragmentation.

Where to find it?
