Ojoche (Brosimum alicastrum)

Characteristics

Large tree with evergreen leaves and edible fruits.

Habitat and distribution

Tropical forests from Mexico to South America.

Behavior

Produces fruits that are a food source for wildlife and humans.

Conservation status

Not threatened.
