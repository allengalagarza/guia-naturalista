White-tailed Deer (Odocoileus virginianus)

Characteristics

Common deer, with a white tail raised when fleeing; up to 80 kg.

Habitat and distribution

From Canada to South America. Present in northern and western Costa Rica.

Behavior

Herbivore, shy, active at dawn and dusk.

Conservation status

Least Concern.
