Tilo (Justicia pectoralis)

Characteristics

Small herb or shrub with aromatic leaves and lilac or pink flowers.

Habitat and distribution

Native to Mesoamerica; common in rural areas, gardens, and hedges.

Medicinal uses

Natural relaxant; relieves insomnia, anxiety, headaches, and colic.

Where to find it?

Grown in home yards, family gardens, or found wild in open areas.

Fun facts

  • In traditional medicine, it is attributed relaxing properties similar to chamomile.
