Spotted Turtle (Rhinoclemmys pulcherrima)

Characteristics

Medium-sized terrestrial turtle with a shell decorated with red and yellow patterns.

Habitat and distribution

Found from Mexico to Costa Rica, inhabiting humid and dry forests, as well as areas near water bodies.

Behavior

Primarily terrestrial but can swim; active day and night; feeds on fruits, leaves, and small invertebrates.

Conservation status

Least Concern (LC) according to the IUCN.
