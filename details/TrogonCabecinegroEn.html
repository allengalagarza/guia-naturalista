Black-headed Trogon (Trogon melanocephalus)

Characteristics

Medium-sized bird with a black head, yellow chest, and green back.

Habitat and distribution

Found in open forests from southern Mexico to northwest Costa Rica.

Behavior

Feeds on fruits and insects; often seen in pairs or small groups.

Conservation status

Least Concern (LC) according to the IUCN.

Where to find it?

Dry and humid forests in northwest Costa Rica. Notable tourist reserves in Nicoya: Karen Mogensen Nature Reserve, Curú Reserve, and Cabo Blanco.

Fun facts

  • Its bright colors and melodious song make it easily recognizable.
