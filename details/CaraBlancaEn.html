White-faced Capuchin Monkey (Cebus capucinus)

Characteristics

White face, dark fur, very agile and intelligent.

Habitat and Distribution

From Honduras to western Ecuador. Costa Rica has healthy populations.

Behavior

They live in large groups (up to 30). Omnivorous and very curious.

Conservation Status

Least Concern, although vulnerable to habitat fragmentation.

Where to find it?

Santa Rosa National Park, Manuel Antonio, Nicoya. Notable tourist reserves in Nicoya: Karen Mogensen Nature Reserve, Curu Reserve, and Cabo Blanco.

Fun Facts

  • They use tools and show advanced social behaviors.
